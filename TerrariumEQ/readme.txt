Here is a code with an acoustic tonestack (Fishman style) for Daisy Seed terrarium
It uses Daisy petal librairy and CMSIS cascaded biquad filter
It requires terrarium.h (from PedalPCB.com)

ON this example
Knob 1 controls the frequency of a highpassfilter (from 10 to 500Hz logscale)
Knob 4 controls the gain of a 110Hz Peak EQ
Knob 5 controls the gain of a 700Hz Peak EQ
Knob 6 controls the gain of a 6.5kHz Shelf EQ
